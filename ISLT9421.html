IS_LT 9421: Usability of Information Systems and Services

This course is an introduction to “concepts and methods of usability testing and research and user-centered design strategy”. This course was taken as part of the Grad Certificate in User Experience and Usability. My goal with this course was to learn how to perform studies that research how to improve the usability of websites and applications.


This course was taken during the Spring 2024 semester with Dr. Noah Glaser. This was an incredibly interesting course, and I learned a lot about usability testing. The course covered topics like heuristic evaluations, accessiblity, and usability as a concept and in practice.

For this course, my group conducted a team project usability study on the usability of the Goodreads website.
