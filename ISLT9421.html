IS_LT 9421: Usability of Information Systems and Services

This course is an introduction to “concepts and methods of usability testing and research and user-centered design strategy”. This course was taken as part of the Grad Certificate in User Experience and Usability. My goal with this course is to learn how to perform studies that research how to improve the usability of websites and applications. I also hope to learn how to implement those changes based on results and findings of the research that will be performed in the class. This will be vital knowledge for my career as an instructional designer. If you do not know what the problems are with a learning tool, then you cannot improve it.


For this course, my group conducted a usability study on the Goodreads website.
