Welcome to my website!

This website serves as a portfolio for courses that I have taken during graduate school. Currently, I am a student in the MS in Learning Technologies and Design program at the University of Missouri. I will discuss the competencies exhibited in each course, as well as present artifacts and reflections that illustrate the competencies and goals for the program. Some courses that I have taken include: ISLT_9421 Usability of Information Systems and Services, IS_LT 9471: Instructional Systems Design, and IS_LT 9480: Internship in Information Science and Learning Technologies. Two of these courses are required for the Graduate Certificate in User Experience and Usability. I have completed the courses required to earn the certificate. This certificate will provide a larger breadth of knowledge and increase my marketability for my goal career as an Instructional Designer.
